HISTORY






This Salai Salai Waghi Fik was established in early 2014. The entrepreneur for the Salai Salai Waghi Fik is one (1) married couple who do business in their own home, which are Mrs. Jamilah Abd Wahab and her husband, Mohd Fikri Bin Hj Jamadi. The chef who cooked the dish for sale was the wife of the owner of Salai Salai Waghi Fik, Mrs. Jamilah Abd Wahab. The business was formed by starting to take orders and deliver to customers and now has the opportunity to do business and give customers and people around the area a taste of village cuisine for their lunch. Initially, the business did not have many customers present and dined at there because they were not aware of the existence of the business but day to day the Salai Salai Waghi Fik dished being talked by the community. So in 2016 they expanded the space for their customers to eat and since then more and more customers have begun to recognize and come to their stall. The cuisine of Negeri Sembilan is very appetizing as the day-to-day customers do not want to miss it and enjoy the dishes they sells. The location of Salai Salai Waghi Fik ialah No. 33, Jalan Kencana 26, Taman Kencana, 56100 Kuala Lumpur. Other than that, Salai Salai Waghi Fik business expanded the contract with the V-Five factory to provide breakfast for the staff of 20 people by 2018. Among the menus for breakfast are Nasi goreng, lempeng. For lunch, one of the dishes is Ayam Masak Merah and the capacity that fit is for 30 people at one (1) time. Finally, new customers would be highly recommend to come to the Salai Waghi Fik to try the Negeri Sembilan cuisine in Kuala Lumpur area.
